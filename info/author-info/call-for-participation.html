Call for Participation

The United Kingdom and Ireland Computing Education Research (UKICER) conference of the two SIGCSE chapters for UK and Ireland (the uki-sigcse.acm.org and the sigcseire.acm.org) is seeking high quality contributions to research relevant to computing science education. The conference proceedings will be published in the ACM Digital Library.

Contributions are sought in a variety of categories with emphasis on high-quality and rigorous work:

  • Papers. Six-page research or tool design papers for presentation at the conference and publication in the proceedings.
  • Posters. Single-page poster proposals for publication in the conference proceedings and poster for presentation at the conference.
  • Doctoral Consortium Applications. Two-page application to participate in the Doctoral Consortium (DC) at the conference, including single-page abstract for publication in the conference proceedings.
  • Critical Research Review (Applications). Two-page application to participate in the Critical Research Review (CRR) at the conference.
  • Workshop proposals. Two-page workshop proposal for workshops to be delivered to attendees at the conference.
  • Research in Practice Project Activities (RIPPA). Two-page proposals for RIPPA activity at conference.

Submission for all these contributions should be made via EasyChair. Authors should consult the detailed submission format instructions and guidelines prior to submitting contributions for consideration. Any questions about submissions should be emailed to the Programme Chairs.
